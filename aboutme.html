About Me

Bio

I have always been driven by a passion for building and creating. Ever since I was a child, I found joy in constructing things. At the age of seven, I built my first house using palm branches for walls and roof, and bamboo raffia as structural elements. This early experience ignited a lifelong fascination with engineering and design.

As a researcher and engineer, I am motivated by the positive impact my work can have on the lives of others. I believe in the power of innovation to create solutions that improve our communities and environments. My guiding motto is simple: respect everyone and cultivate love for myself and others. This philosophy fuels my dedication to my vocation.

Profile

I am a proud Associate Fellow of the Higher Education Academy (AFHEA), committed to enhancing the learning experience of my students. My professional journey has been enriched by various experiences, including teaching and research that prioritize student engagement and success.

Personal Development

I am passionate about personal and professional growth, participating in events such as the "Supporting Emotionally Unstable Students" workshop and the "Excellent Teachers on Teaching Excellence" Symposium 2024. Additionally, I attended the Education and Student Development Salon on "How to Make the Best Choice in Life?" and have undergone specialized training in university teaching methods.

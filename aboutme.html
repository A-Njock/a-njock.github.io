About Me

Bio

Since childhood, I've been passionate about building and creating. At the age of seven, I constructed my first house using palm branches and bamboo raffia. This sparked a lifelong interest in engineering and design.

As a researcher and engineer, I'm dedicated to making a positive impact through innovation. My guiding motto is to respect everyone and cultivate love for myself and others.
